This project uses a structured dataset containing individual-level records on education, employment status, and monthly income.
  
The primary table, "people", includes:
- person_id: unique identifier
- education_level: highest level of education attained
- employment_status: current employment status
- monthly_income: reported monthly income
  
The dataset was validated through schema inspection and record sampling prior to analysis.
Raw data is not included in this repository.
